My work explores the relationship between multiculturalism and multimedia experiences. With influences as diverse as Kierkegaard and Roy Lichtenstein, new synergies are distilled from both constructed and discovered textures.

Ever since I was a postgraduate I have been fascinated by the traditional understanding of the human condition. What starts out as contemplation soon becomes finessed into a manifesto of distress, leaving only a sense of dread and the inevitability of a new reality.

As shifting phenomena become distorted through diligent and academic practice, the viewer is left with an insight into the

limits
of our world.
